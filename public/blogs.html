Vesicoscopic Ureteric Reimplantation

Vesicoureteric reflux is the medical term for a health condition where the patient experiences an abnormal flow of urine. The urine starts to flow from the bladder back to the ureters. Usually, urine flow is unidirectional in the human body, but when affected by this condition, it becomes bidirectional. The condition develops due to a urinary tract infection.
