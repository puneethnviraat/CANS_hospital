Emergency

Serving as a rapid response team, CANS emergency department is staffed 24 hours a day with in-house trauma surgeons, anesthesiologists, trauma nurses and vital support services.

The Trauma Care Unit is fully equipped with 10 beds, including triage beds and observation areas, to treat all forms of medical, surgical, cardiology, polytrauma, pediatric and neurological conditions. CANS Hospital Emergency Department staff strives to provide patients with the highest quality emergency care and services in an efficient and timely manner.
