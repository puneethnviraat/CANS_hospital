About Me:

Dr. Niranthara Ganesh Did His Mbbs At Ramaiah Medical College, Bangalore , and his Masters In Orthopaedics And Traumatology At World -renowned Ramaiah Medical College and Hospitals. After Returning From Abroad, from fellowship in Joint Replacement surgeries, (Arthroplasty Surgeon) He Spent Six Years As A Orthopaedic surgeon At Ramaiah Hospitals , Bangalore.

After Which He Is been practicing in Ramaiah Hospitals as A Joint Replacement Surgeon and Complex Trauma surgeon . Dr Niranthara Ganesh Holds Basic And Intermediate certifications Of The Ao Course, Advanced life and trauma support courses .

He has conducted more than 500 free health camps in the rural and urban sectors of Karnataka ,1000 free surgeries and been actively helping the needy in the flood affected regions of Chennai in 2015 and North Karnataka in 2019. His Particular Areas Of Interest Are In Hip And Knee Replacement, Traumatology, Shoulder And Knee Arthroscopies, Spine Surgeries And Back Care, Limb Lengthening Procedures And In Sports Medicine.

He knows: English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil and Telugu.
