About Me:

Dr. Nikhil C Hire math is a Consultant Neurologist at SPARSH Hospital, Bangalore. Dr. Nikhil is an expert clinical neurologist and stroke specialist who is strong in his commitments to his work. Dr. Nikhil completed his graduation from JJM Medical College, Davanagere and then pursued MD in General Medicine from Karnataka Institute of Medical Sciences, Hubli, Karnataka. Additionally, he has done Doctorate of Medicine (DM) in Neurology and has completed Post-Doctoral Fellowship in Stroke Comprehensive Stroke Care Program from Sree Chitra Tirumala Institute for medical sciences and Technology(SCTIMST) Trivandrum. In addition to stroke he is interested in epilepsy and neuromuscular disorders. He has a number of articles in peer-reviewed journals to his credits. He has been awarded young investigator award from World stroke congress in October 2016. Dr. Nikhil is also a member of Indian Academy of Neurology and Indian Stroke Association.
