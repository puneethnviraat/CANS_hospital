About Me:

Dr Chaitra S Niranthara is a Obsterterian and gynaecologist specialised in Reproductive Immunology and is an Alumnus of M S Ramaiah medical college. She was awarded the Sri P Nagesam Award for the best outgoing Postgraduate and topper in M S OBG. As someone who believes in a synergy of mind, body, and soul as the ultimate outcome of patient care, she is part of Chanre immunology centre. Her areas of expertise include Reproductive immunology, infertility treatment, PCOS, Counselling, and high-risk pregnancy.
Awards and Accolades: Third Prize for poster presentation in Gnanavarsha , second place for the best original research paper at KSOGA. She has received many prizes in debates, quiz competitions and poster presentations at various conferences. She is also received the Karnataka state award by the health family welfare department in June 2019 and The Times of India award October 2019. Along with her clinical work, she is actively involved in planning and executing training programs, giving lectures for students, clinicians, and research.

She knows: English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil and Telugu.
