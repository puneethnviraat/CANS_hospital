Obstetrics and Gynaecology

Obstetrics and Gynaecology are two separate branches of medicine that focus exclusively on health conditions related to women. While Obstetrics looks into pregnancy and childbirth, Gynaecology focuses on issues related to a woman’s reproductive system. Medical experts who look exclusively into these two conditions are referred to as Obstetricians and Gynaecologists respectively.

While both are different specialities, an experienced medical expert in these two fields can contribute as an obstetrician as well as a gynaecologist.

  • Hysteroscopy

    This is a procedure which is performed to check abnormal uterine bleeding. An instrument called Hysteroscope is used by doctors and is inserted through the vagina into the uterus. This process also helps doctors look out for fibroids and septums. Hysteroscopy is a safe procedure except for some minor reactions that might occur due to the stress while undergoing the procedure. These can be cured with medicines.

  • PCOD/PCOS Treatment

    Polycystic Ovarian Disorder (PCOD) and Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) are commonly observed conditions in women. While PCOD can occur due to hormonal imbalance, PCOS relates to the endocrine system. PCOD and PCOS if left untreated, can cause further health complications like diabetes, obesity, heart disease, and high cholesterol. It might also affect pregnancy. Hence it would be good to seek the opinion of your consultant gynaecologist in Bangalore immediately upon observing any symptom.
