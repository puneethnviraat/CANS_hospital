Physiotherapy

Physiotherapy is a discipline of science concerned with the treatment, prevention, and rehabilitation of physically handicapped individuals.

At CANS we will be taking care of an individual's physical and muscular health.

Benefits CANS Hospital:

  • Pain management with reduced need for opioids
  • Avoiding surgery
  • Recovery from injury or trauma
  • Recovery from stroke or paralysis
  • Fall prevention
