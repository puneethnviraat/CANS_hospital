About Me:

Graduated MBBS from JNMC Belgaum in the year 1983. joined NIMHANS as resident in Neurology Department in 1984 and worked for 1 year. Had an opportunity to go to USA and settle in 1985. Returned back to India and joined MD Anaesthesiology in KMC Mangalore from 1989 to 1992. After completion of PG I worked as specialist in Sanjay Gandhi Accident Hospital in the department of Anaesthesia and ICU for 2 years. From 1994 to 2004 worked in my hometown Chikmagalur as HOD of Anaesthesiology in a 400 bedded hospital- Mallegowda General Hospital. 2004-2006 Returened back to Bangalore and started working as consultant Anaesthesiologist And Intensivist in Corporate hospitals. 2006-2012 had an opportunity to work in kingdom of Saudi Arabia as specialist Anaesthesiologist and Intensivist in Zizan Province. Here I came across several diseases which I had heard and read in books. Treating patients of the Arabian and African race was overall a very good experience.
