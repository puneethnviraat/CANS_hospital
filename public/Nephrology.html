Nephrology

Our renal team is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure and technical equipment and is supported by a dedicated and talented team of nephrologists, renal transplant surgeons, anaesthesiologists, critical care physicians and renal pathologists. I'm here.

The Nephrology Center provides affordable, high-quality diagnosis and treatment for all types of kidney-related disorders. We offer a wide range of treatment techniques and support, including haemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis.
