Plastic reconstructive & aesthetic surgery

CANS' plastic, reconstructive and cosmetic surgery team provides the best possible outcome for each patient, every time. We are committed to providing patients with a wide range of advanced care for both common and specific problems in the field of cosmetic and reconstructive surgery. The center is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment for diagnosis and treatment in a calm and private environment.

Specialities in CANS Hospital:

  • skin grafts
  • skin flap surgery
  • tissue expansion
  • fat transfer or grafting
  • vacuum closure
  • camouflage make-up or cream
