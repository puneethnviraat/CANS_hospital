General surgery and alied specialities

General Surgery is a speciality of medicine where medical experts not only carry out surgeries pertaining to common ailments like hernia, appendicitis and gall bladder removal to name a few but also look into the post-surgical medical care of the patients. Surgical Excellence has been our speciality at the Department of General Surgery at CANS Hospital rated as the best hospital for General Surgery in Bangalore.

The most critical hour is the time when you need not just expertise but patience and skill to address a problem through surgical intervention if required. This is where our efficient and vastly experienced General Surgeons at CANS Hospital have made a considerable difference. With a wealth of experience and excellent infrastructure to help you, we are there when you need us at the most critical time.

Why choose CANS Hospital for General Surgery ?

It is not just the skill sets of the surgeons that matter. Equally important is the post-operative care phase where the actual recovery of the patient commences. Our efficient surgeons continue to display exemplary patient care with a compassionate approach towards each and every patient who visits our hospital. This is why they have been regarded as the most trusted general surgeons in Bangalore.

CANS Hospital is widely spoken about as the best Superspeciality Hospital in Bangalore for its aesthetically designed Operation Theatres with advanced technology-driven surgical equipment. We take complete care in fumigating the theatres at regular intervals to keep them hygienically perfect. There is zero compromise on following all the standard operating procedures and hygiene, as laid out by the hospital management.

Our Strength continues to be the presence of the most versatile team of surgeons distributed across different disciplines. CANS Hospital is well-known for its high percentage of success rate for critical surgeries and hence, is the most preferred and trusted hospital by patients for General Surgery in Bangalore
