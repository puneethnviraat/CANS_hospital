Orthopedic

Our orthopaedic department provides comprehensive and top-notch orthopaedic services. Our expert team provides specialized care in the areas of complex trauma, Polytrauma, and other sub-areas of orthopaedics. It provides treatment for all diseases of the bones, joints, and various musculoskeletal disorders.

The department has extensive experience in diagnosing and treating upper and lower extremity surgery, trauma, bone disease, and congenital disorders. In addition to our highly qualified orthopaedic staff, we also work with highly qualified occupational therapists, physical therapists, and pain management specialists.

Specialities in CANS Hospital:

  • Arthroplasty
  • Arthroscope of knee
  • Shoulder Surgery
  • Sports Injury
  • Regenerative medicine with special emphasis on stem cell based cartilage repair
