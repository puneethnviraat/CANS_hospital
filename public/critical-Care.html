Critical care

An immediate, life-threatening condition can fall upon us at any moment and without prior notice. This is when instant medical attention, without the slightest delay, becomes the need of the hour.

A Critical Care Unit is a department that is specifically built with the objective to serve people needing immediate medical attention and stabilize the patient and maintain normal body functioning.
Critical Care Centres in Bangalore are well-equipped with doctors and nursing staff who are specifically trained to meet unforeseen medical situations. Additionally, they are also equipped with the basic medical infrastructure needed to stabilize the patient immediately.

Why choose CANS Hospital for Critical Care Attention?

CANS Hospital is built exclusively with the objective of providing medical care of the highest quality to all our patients. All our Intensive Care Units are designed with patient comfort in mind. Our intensive care unit is winning the trust of many patients due to its approach towards a patient in distress. CANS Hospital is rated as one of the best Multispeciality hospitals in Bangalore for Critical Care.

The Intensive Care units at the hospital are a potent combination of the most modern medical equipment and medical excellence in the form of vastly experienced doctors and nurses. No stone is left unturned by our efficient team in attending to the patient needing attention in the most comfortable and safe way.

All the necessary critical care arrangements are overseen on a day-to-day basis by our HOD-Critical Care and periodic audits are conducted to ensure that we are ever alert to meet any emergency situation. CANS Hospital has successfully seen through critical cases of varied complexities including those coming in with serious cardiac issues, post-transplant issues, trauma patients and stroke patients.
