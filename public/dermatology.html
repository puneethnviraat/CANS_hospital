  • Laser Hair Removal

    This is a medical procedure recommended by dermatologists to remove excess/ unwanted hair from different parts of the body. The procedure makes use of a Laser beam to remove unwanted hair. The success of Laser hair removal as a treatment procedure depends more on the skin type and the colour of the hair. Some of the best dermatologists in Bangalore are a part of the Department of Dermatology and are well-trained in laser treatment procedures.

  • PRP, Face & Hair

    When it comes to technology-driven, precision-quality skin care, CANS Hospital, the well-known dermatology hospital in Bangalore, leads by example. The PRP or Platelet Rich Plasma method is a proven method for cosmetic rejuvenation and is successfully employed to remove wrinkles and scars from the face. This process involves drawing a small quantity of blood, usually from your arm and then separating the plate-rich plasma from the blood by putting it into a centrifuge. This plasma is then injected back into the body through the area the blood was drawn from to facilitate hair growth. PRP is a safe procedure and usually does not lead to any complications.

  • Derma Roll

    Derma roll is a type of skin treatment which uses a device called a Derma roller to rejuvenate your skin. It is an effective treatment which also reduces signs of ageing. Derma Roll has gained a lot of popularity and acceptance in recent years due to it being a completely safe and non-invasive procedure. This treatment makes the skin firmer and also removes any traces of wrinkles if formed.

  • Tag removal

    Skin tags are commonly observed and usually resemble your skin in appearance. However, they are harmless as they are non-cancerous. Most of them do not need treatment but if you come across any symptom including bleeding, it is good to seek the advice of a skin care specialist in Bangalore.

  • Eczema Treatment

    Eczema is a type of Dermatitis (a condition causing skin inflammation) which is usually not contagious. However, if ignored it can lead to more serious complications.   • Skin Biopsy

    Skin Biopsy is a test procedure used to diagnose critical skin infections, skin cancers or even the cause of rashes.
