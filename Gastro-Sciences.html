Gastro sciences

Aster's Gastroenterology Medical Center has state-of-the-art equipment supported by state-of-the-art intensive care units. Equipped with high-tech diagnostic facilities for superior diagnosis of pediatric and adult gastrointestinal and hepatobiliary diseases, our gastroenterologists provide expert care with the full spectrum of gastroenterology.

Gastroenterologists at Aster Hospitals provide clinical services worldwide for the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the gastrointestinal tract and liver. Our gastroenterologists examine and treat complex and chronic gastrointestinal, pancreatic, liver and gallbladder ailments.

The center provides physicians with a state-of-the-art infrastructure supported by world-class technology to support physicians in their pursuit of medical excellence. Aster Hospitals is well known for its multidisciplinary collaboration, bringing together India's leading experts to provide world-class effective gastric care.
